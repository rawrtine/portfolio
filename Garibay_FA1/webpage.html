Books Recommendation

Seventh Sense
by Purpleyhan

As the next head of the Shinigami tribe, Rielle was given a mission to infiltrate their archenemy's base, Tantei High, in search of any information about the legendary seventh sense. Her animosity toward the Senshins made it hard for her to get closer to her goal, and worse, Hideo, the heir to the Senshin's tribe seat of power, had already deemed Rielle suspicious.

Tantei High
by Purpleyhan

Infamous as a latecomer, Rainie found herself expelled from her current school due to her unpunctuality. Her seemingly ordinary life began to take a strange turn when she was forced to enroll in an unknown and suspicious institution located in the middle of a forest.

My Signature Move
by Cassandra Diviak

A sweet, rivals-to-lovers romance with a sneaky emotional punch. The balance between the sports. both ice hockey and figure skating.

Loveboat, Taipei
by Abigail Hing Wen

When eighteen-year-old Ever Wong parents send her from Ohio to Taiwan to study Mandarin for the summer, she finds herself thrust among the very over-achieving kids her parents have always wanted her to be, including Rick Woo, the Yale-bound prodigy profiled in the Chinese newspapers since they were nine—and her parents yardstick for her never-measuring-up life.
